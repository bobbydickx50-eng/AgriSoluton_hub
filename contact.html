Frequently Asked Questions

Is this a real agricultural service?

AgriSolutions Hub is an educational project developed by a student at Sokoine University of Agriculture. While it demonstrates real agricultural solutions, it's primarily for educational purposes.

Can I actually buy/sell products here?

The platform simulates agricultural trading functionality. For real trading, please contact established agricultural markets in Tanzania.

Are the market prices real-time?

Market prices are simulated for educational purposes. For real-time prices, please refer to official Tanzania agricultural market reports.

How accurate is the weather information?

Weather data is simulated. For accurate weather forecasts, please check Tanzania Meteorological Authority (TMA) official reports.

Can I get agricultural advice from experts?

While this platform provides educational resources, for professional agricultural advice, please contact SUA extension services or Tanzania's Ministry of Agriculture.

Is this project for commercial use?

This is an educational project submitted for academic assessment. Commercial use would require further development and regulatory approvals.
