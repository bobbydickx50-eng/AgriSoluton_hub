About AgriSolutions Hub

Empowering Tanzanian farmers through technology and education

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide Tanzanian farmers with accessible, reliable agricultural information and connect them to global markets through innovative technology solutions.

Our Vision

To become East Africa's leading digital agricultural platform, transforming farming into a profitable and sustainable business for all stakeholders.

Our History

AgriSolutions Hub was conceptualized in 2024 as an educational project by David Bablo Mushii, a Second Year Diploma student in Information Technology at Sokoine University of Agriculture.

The project aims to bridge the digital divide in Tanzania's agricultural sector by providing farmers with technology-driven solutions for market access, information sharing, and business growth.
